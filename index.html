The Future of Housing is Intergenerational

Ireland faces a severe housing shortage, with students particularly affected. Average rents in Dublin exceed €1,400/month, while 38,900 students lack adequate housing.

Meanwhile, over 250,000 spare bedrooms sit empty in homes across Dublin, many belonging to seniors living alone.

Intergenerational home-sharing offers a win-win: affordable rent for students, companionship and supplemental income for seniors. Programs worldwide have shown success rates above 85%.

LifeMatch is bringing this solution to Ireland with our compatibility-first matching approach.
